    Units

    • Settler: Used to found new cities.
    • Warrior: Basic combat unit, capable of attacking enemies.
    • Hero: A powerful unit that gains experience, levels up, and unlocks new abilities.

    Gameplay

    • Movement: Select a unit and press (m) or click the 'Move' button to highlight movable tiles. Click a highlighted tile to move the unit.
    • Attack (Warrior/Hero): Select a unit and press (a) or click the 'Attack' button to highlight enemies within range. Click a highlighted enemy to attack. Moving a unit onto an enemy tile will also initiate an attack.
    • Settle City (Settler): Select a Settler and press (s) or click the 'Settle City' button to found a new city at the unit's location.
    • Skip Action: Press space or click the 'Skip Action' button to end a unit's turn without performing an action.
